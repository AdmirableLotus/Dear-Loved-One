About Us

Lotus is a unique journal based in Omaha, NE, designed to help individuals create heartfelt letters and notes for their loved ones before their passing. Our platform allows users to set up the date of release of the journal, ensuring that their messages reach their loved ones at the right time.

At Lotus, we understand the importance of leaving a lasting legacy and expressing one's deepest emotions. Our mission is to provide a safe and meaningful space for individuals to preserve their thoughts, memories, and messages of love, ensuring that their words continue to inspire and bring comfort to their loved ones even after they are gone.
